July 1–11, 2025

Focus: Culture, Comfort, and Shellfish-Free Dining

Milan (July 1–3)

  • Sforza Castle Museum: €5 – Piazza Castello, Milan

Restaurants (Shellfish-Free)

  • L’Osteria del Binari: Cotoletta Milanese, €25–€35

Restaurants (Shellfish-Free)

Restaurants (Shellfish-Free)

Naples (July 7–10)

Scheduled Tours

  • Pompeii + Vesuvius Day Tour: €85–€100

Restaurants (Shellfish-Free)

  • Sorbillo Pizzeria: Margherita pizza, €8–€12
  • Tandem Ragù: Slow-cooked meat sauces, €15–€25

Useful Italian Phrases

  • "Siamo allergiche ai crostacei." – We are allergic to shellfish.
  • "Niente frutti di mare, per favore." – No seafood, please.
  • "Dove il bagno?" – Where is the bathroom?
  • "Il conto, per favore." – The check, please.
